Who We Are

Four Pillars of Rise Up

We believe in growing the whole person — spirit, soul, and body. Our community is built on these core values.

Faith

Deepening our walk with God through authentic worship, personal devotion, and community prayer. We anchor everything in scripture.

Fellowship

Building genuine, lifelong friendships through shared experiences, support, and connection. No one walks alone here.

Fitness

Honoring God with our bodies through football, sports, and physical wellness. We play hard and play with purpose.

Leadership

Developing the next generation of servant-leaders who lead with integrity, courage, and compassion in every area of life.
